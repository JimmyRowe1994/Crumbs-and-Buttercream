WHO WE ARE

Ryan Smith & Eddy Hankins - Bakers and Founders

Hello, I'm Ryan from Crumbs & Buttercream. Please let me give you a little overview of myself and our business: Crumbs & Buttercream was formed by myself, Ryan, and my partner, Eddy, in early 2018. However, I have been baking since I was a child and I'm absolutely in love with it. I pride myself on my work and have worked extremely hard to get to where I am now, and can only hope with continued support from old and new customers, Crumbs & Buttercream will continue to grow.

When it comes to my baking, this is done daily for the orders that are in, and I never cook and freeze my cakes; cakes are baked freshly within 24 hours of leaving me and going to you. I always advise that cakes are best enjoyed within 48 hours if not kept in the box provided, otherwise they are best enjoyed within 4 days. Never store cakes or cupcakes in the fridge or in any airtight Tupperware.

Established in 2018 we have grown from our early days in Penryn to now supply cakes across Cornwall and the rest of Great Britain, from 5-star luxury hotels to a marquee in the middle of the fabulous Cornwall countryside.

Our cake studio is situated on the outside of the historic town of Penryn, only a short stroll from the fabulously stunning fishing town of Falmouth. It's a place of light, peacefullness and inspiration. From there we are able to create luxury wedding and celebration cakes and deliver them to fabulous venues across Cornwall and Great Britain.

In our mind we count it an honour to work with each client to design and craft a cake that truly reflects their unique occasion or celebration. Naturally, the Crumbs & Buttercream team offer our services to the highest level.
