Delivering Your Cake

Delivery charge is calculated on a mileage to and from the studio and time basis. Alternatively, cakes can be collected from our studio in Penryn.

If you choose delivery, it is our intention that your cake will be delivered in perfect condition prior to the start of your reception.

However, we ask for patience with factors that may be beyond our control (e.g. traffic and weather conditions ) as we cannot be held liable in the rare occasion of any such delays. In some circumstances, we may recommend the cake is delivered 1 day in advance.

It is your responsibility to ensure you inform us of your correct ceremony and reception time plus the postcode of the venue.

In the unlikely event of late delivery, the maximum compensation will be a refund of the delivery price.

In the extremely unlikely event of serious damage due to unforeseen circumstances (e.g. car accident during delivery that destroys the cake) we will endeavour to provide a replacement display cake for your cake cutting photo and the wedding breakfast.

We cannot be held liable for any damage that is rendered to the cake at the venue once we have left the premises. Cake most not be stored in a fridge or cold room.

If you choose to collect the cake, we cannot be held liable for any damage to the cake once it has left our studio. Tiered cakes are most at risk during transport, so please seek our advice to transport it for you to your venue and set up cake as per design.

If any damage does occur to the cake whilst in your possession, we would be happy to rectify the damage where possible. However it is your responsibility to ensure the cake is returned to us to enable the repair work to take place and a cost will occur.

Can't collected your cake to whatever reason, late requests for delivery (after securing the date and booking for a collection) will be accommodated where possible, but cannot be guaranteed as earlier delivery commitments will take priority.

Pricing

Celebration Cakes

The price of each cake we create is based on a number of factors: The size, the number of servings, the complexity of the design, and style. Smaller, simply decorated cakes cost less than larger, more highly decorated cakes which require a greater use of expertise and time to design and create. (We operate on a minimum of 20 servings in a cake; please bare this in mind when planning your cake, although we do offer mini cakes with a minimum of 12 servings.)

Celebration Cakes

Starting from £60, our Celebration Cakes are simply covered in fondant and decorated in simple but beautiful ways.

Sculpted Cakes

Starting from £100, these are cakes that have a sculpted topper, sugar flower or are sculpted into a shape and are small to medium in size and require little or no internal support.

Mini Cakes

Starting from £6, these are cakes that are great to share or for one person to indulge themselves in. Minimum order applies.

Cutting Cakes

Starting from £50, these are large tray cakes covered in fondant ready for cutting. No decorations.

Wedding Cakes

The same as the celebration cakes, the price of wedding cakes we create is based on a number of factors: The size, the number of servings, the complexity of design, and style. Smaller, simply decorated cakes cost less than larger, more highly decorated cakes which require a greater use of expertise, tme and design consultation to create. (We operate on a minimum of 20 servings in a cake; please bare this in mind when planning your cake.)

Adore Collection

Starting from £200 and 1 or 2 tiers, our Adore Collection is simply covered and decorated with an impressive but yet simple finish. Adore designs are selected from 6 elegant designs, previous work, or agreed via detailed description over email or telephone.

Elegance Collection

Starting from £350 and 3+ tiers, our Elegance Collection has a greater amount of flourish, finish and sugar-work than our other cakes.

Charm Collection

Starting from £500, our Charm Collection are cakes that have a greater amount of technical complexity, piping, sugar art and finishes, along with our signature flavours and unique designs.

Exquisite Collection

Starting from £700, our Exquisite Collection requires the highest amount of technical expertise in terms of size, structure and complexity of the finished design. This collection typically involves sugar art, detailed, handcrafted finishes and intricate piping.

For cakes from our Elegance, Charms and Exquisite collections, we are please to offer hour-lasting tasting and consultation sessions for up to four people with a member of the Crumbs & Buttercream team, as well as a second session for cakes from our Charms and Exquisite collections. Consultation fees will be deducted from any order subsequently placed.

Sizing

Sizes and portions of cakes depend on how the cake is cut. Below is a guide which will be with the cake on delivery so that the person cutting the cake will know how many portions they should get out of the cake. All wedding cakes are delivered by us and set up in the venue. Usually, sponge cakes are cut into 2" x 1" slices, which is known as a dessert portion, and fruit cakes into 1" x 1" slices, which is known as a finger portion.
